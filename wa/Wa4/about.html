About Me

Background

I am a student with an interest in web design, interaction design, and digital storytelling. I enjoy learning how structure and accessibility influence how people experience websites. Rather than focusing only on how things look, I value designing systems that are intuitive, meaningful, and usable by a wide range of audiences.

Interests and Goals

My goals include continuing to improve my technical skills in HTML and CSS while gaining a deeper understanding of accessibility and user-centered design. I am especially interested in projects that blend creativity with problem solving and empathy. Long term, I hope to work on digital products that have a positive impact on users.
