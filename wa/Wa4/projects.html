Academic Work

Many of my projects come from coursework focused on web design and digital media. These assignments emphasize semantic HTML, accessibility, and thoughtful content organization. Rather than visual complexity, my work prioritizes clarity, structure, and ease of navigation for users.

Creative Exploration

In addition to academic projects, I enjoy experimenting with creative ideas such as layout planning, information hierarchy, and storytelling through content. These projects help me better understand how users move through digital spaces and how design decisions affect comprehension and engagement.
